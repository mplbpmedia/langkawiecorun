Post-Event Review

From Vision to Impact: Langkawi Eco Run 2025

Langkawi Eco Run 2025 marked a historic milestone as the first edition fully organised by Majlis Perbandaran Langkawi Bandaraya Pelancongan (MPLBP). The event received an encouraging response, attracting approximately 480 participants from various states across Malaysia. Held under the theme “Stride for Sustainability”, the run was designed to promote environmental awareness, healthy living, and community engagement while supporting MPLBP’s vision of creating a “Cleaner, Greener, Smarter” Langkawi. Through the power of sports, participants were encouraged to embrace sustainable practices and contribute towards preserving the island’s natural beauty for future generations.

The event successfully combined environmental responsibility with community empowerment. A No Plastic Bag initiative was implemented throughout the programme, while participants received unique finisher medals crafted from recycled wood materials, symbolising the commitment towards sustainability. To further enhance the experience, MPLBP organised an exciting lucky draw featuring more than 40 attractive prizes, including electronic devices, gadgets, and sports equipment. In addition, ten business booths were allocated to B40 entrepreneurs and local small-scale traders, providing valuable opportunities to generate income and showcase their products to visitors and participants.

The overwhelmingly positive feedback received from participants reflected the success of Langkawi Eco Run 2025 as more than just a running event—it became a platform for promoting sustainability, supporting local communities, and fostering a healthier lifestyle. Encouraged by the strong response and numerous requests from runners to continue the programme, MPLBP is proud to bring back the event this year with even greater excitement, enhanced experiences, and new challenges through Langkawi Eco Run 2.0, continuing the journey towards a cleaner, greener, and smarter future.
