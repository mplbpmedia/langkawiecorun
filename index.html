Our Mission

Stride For
Sustainability

Langkawi Eco Run 2.0 is more than just a competitive running event, it is a community movement dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of our legendary island. Guided by the principles of environmental sustainability, community engagement, and healthy living, the event embraces the theme “Stride for Sustainability,” where every step represents a commitment towards a better future. Aligned with the vision of Majlis Perbandaran Langkawi Bandaraya Pelancongan and its slogan “Cleaner, Greener, Smarter,” Langkawi Eco Run 2.0 aims to inspire environmental awareness, promote sustainable lifestyles, and unite the community in building a greener and more sustainable Langkawi. 🌿

Cleaner

Our commitment to a cleaner Langkawi is reflected through litter-free running zones, and sustainability awareness initiatives that encourage participants and the community to reduce waste and protect the island’s natural environment.

Greener

Every stride contributes to a greener future. Through eco-friendly practices, sustainable event management, and environmental conservation efforts, we aim to promote a deeper appreciation for Langkawi’s natural heritage while encouraging a more sustainable lifestyle for all.

Smarter

Integration of technology in efficient event management, digital registration systems, and accurate race timing to enhance the overall participant experience.
