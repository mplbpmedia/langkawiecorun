RULES & REGULATIONS

Langkawi Eco Run 2.0

Participants are required to follow the rules and regulations below to ensure a safe and enjoyable event for everyone.

01 / Participation Rules

  • Participants must ensure they are medically and physically fit to participate.
  • Participants take part in the event at their own risk.
  • Age restrictions (if any) must be strictly followed as stated during registration.

02 / Race Conduct

  • Participants must follow the designated running route at all times.
  • Cutting or deviating from the official route is strictly discouraged.
  • Unsportsmanlike behaviour, including pushing, obstructing, or aggressive conduct, is not allowed.

03 / Safety Compliance

  • Participants must follow instructions given by event marshals, volunteers, and organisers.
  • For safety reasons, participants must be aware of traffic conditions at road crossings.
  • Proper running attire and footwear are recommended.

04 / Personal Belongings

Participants are responsible for their own belongings during the event. The organiser will not be responsible for any loss, theft, or damage of personal items.

05 / Environmental Responsibility

Participants are heavily encouraged to maintain cleanliness throughout the event. To protect Langkawi's ecosystem, please follow these strict rules:

Littering along the route is strictly prohibited. Participants must not litter.
Participants are required to keep the environment clean throughout the entire route.
Any form of waste must be disposed of properly at designated eco-bins prepared at water stations.

06 / Event Authority

  • The organiser reserves the right to remove any participant who violates the rules or poses a safety risk.
  • The organiser’s decision on all matters related to the event is final.

07 / Changes to Event

The organiser reserves the right to amend rules, route, or event arrangements if necessary for safety, weather compliance, or overall operational reasons.

08 / Code of Conduct

Participants must behave respectfully at all times during the event. Any inappropriate, disruptive, or indecent behaviour is strictly prohibited.

Note: The organiser reserves the right to remove any participant who violates conduct rules immediately without any refund.

09 / Refund Policy

All registration fees are strictly non-refundable.

No refunds will be provided for cancellations, no-shows, or the inability to participate for any given reason including sudden weather shifts or personal schedule conflicts.

Final Acceptance

By participating in Langkawi Eco Run 2.0, all participants are deemed to have read, understood, and agreed to these rules and regulations.
