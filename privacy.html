PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 2026

Langkawi Eco Run 2.0 ("the Event") respects and protects the privacy of all participants. This Privacy Policy explains how participant information is collected, used, and stored.

01 / Information We Collect

When registering for the Event, participants may be required to provide:

  • Full Name
  • ID Number / Passport Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Contact Number
  • Email Address
  • Emergency Contact Information

02 / Purpose of Data Collection

Participant information is collected for the following purposes:

✓

Event registration and participant verification

✓

Communication regarding the Event

✓

Preparation of participant lists and race materials

✓

Emergency and safety management during the Event

✓

Event administration and operational purposes

03 / Data Protection

All participant information will be handled responsibly and used only for purposes related to the Event. Reasonable measures will be taken to protect personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.

04 / Disclosure of Information

Participant information will not be sold or shared with third parties except when required for Event operations, safety management, or when required by law.

05 / Photography and Media

By participating in the Event, participants acknowledge that photographs, videos, and recordings may be taken during the Event and may be used by the organizer for promotional, marketing, publicity, or reporting purposes without further notice or compensation.

06 / Policy Updates

The organizer reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any updates will be published on the official Event website.

07 / Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, participants may contact the Event organizer through the official communication channels provided on the website.
