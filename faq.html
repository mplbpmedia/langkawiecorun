Community Runner's Guide

General Information • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Registration & Categories

Unfortunately, category changes are not allowed once registration is confirmed. This helps our community committee smoothly manage t-shirt size orders and bib number allocations.

There is no age limit! The 5KM category is fully family-friendly. Children under 12 must be accompanied by a registered parent or guardian. Baby strollers are also highly welcome on the route.

Any t-shirt size amendments can only be requested via email within 3 days of your registration. After this grace period, your jersey will be printed strictly according to your initial choice.

Yes, slot transfers are allowed up to 2 weeks before the event. Participant information can be updated by contacting our support team via WhatsApp.

All event registrations are non-refundable. If you can't make it, we highly suggest utilizing our slot transfer option to pass your entry ticket to a friend instead.

2. Race Kit Collection

As a grassroots community run, we do not provide courier services. All race kits must be collected physically at the designated venue on Thursday and Friday prior to race day.

Absolutely! Your representative must present your confirmation email (QR Code) and a signed Authorization Letter which can be easily downloaded on our Info Page.

No. Registration and collection booths close completely on Friday evening so our volunteer team can fully shift their focus to final track and site preparations for Saturday morning.

Every registered runner receives a Drawstring Bag containing your Official Running Jersey, a Race Bib, snacks and mineral water.

Jersey size exchanges on collection days are subject to left-over stock availability only. To avoid disappointment, please check your size carefully during online registration.

3. Event Day & Logistics

Open parking space is available completely FREE of charge around the main venue (Dataran Tanjung Rhu, Langkawi). Please arrive early before 6:30 AM to avoid local traffic bottlenecks.

Yes, a complimentary baggage storage tent opens at 6:30 AM. Please refrain from storing high-value belongings like laptops, heavy cash, or expensive jewelry. Pihak urus setia is not liable for structural loss.

We strongly encourage you to wear it to amplify the eco-community spirit! However, the only ABSOLUTELY MANDATORY item to display prominently at all times is your Official Race Bib.

In case of light drizzle, the run continues. If extreme weather hits (lightning or severe wind), the flag-off will be temporarily delayed for safety under civil defense/police guidance.

Yes, hydration points are stationed roughly every 2KM to 5KM. They will serve mineral water and basic energy drinks to ensure all community runners stay safely hydrated.

4. Sustainability & Eco Rules

To honor our green mission, throwing plastic cups, bottles or energy gel packets casually on village paths is strictly prohibited. Waste must be dropped into trash meshes located 50m after each water station.

Yes! Every single runner in both 5KM and 10KM formats who crosses the finish arch before the official Cut-Off Time will receive a high-quality medal made of eco-recycled materials.

Stop running immediately and alert the closest track marshall (identified by neon green vests). Mobile medics on motorbikes are patrolling the route constantly to provide aid.

For the collective safety and comfort of all village runners, pets are not allowed on the active running tracks. Let's keep it clean and comfortable for everyone!

No, certificates are not provided for this event. No physical prints will be distributed, saving paper and staying true to our green ethos!
